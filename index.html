Loan Repayment vs. Investment Calculator

This versatile calculator allows you to compare the financial outcomes of paying off loans, including mortgages, against investing over a specified period. It's ideal for evaluating the best use of your funds between loan amortization and investment opportunities.

Net Financial Benefit measures the financial impact of investing versus loan repayment, including both investment returns and interest savings. It accounts for the total interest paid on loans, offering a clear picture of each option's effect on your net worth over time.

Wealth Warning: Markets are volatile. Rates and returns will not be smooth like in this tool. Actual outcomes can significantly vary, impacting the financial benefits of these strategies.
